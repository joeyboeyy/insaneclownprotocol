How To Buy

Create a wallet

Create an ICP Internet Identity, or create one of the following wallets: Metamask (MSQ), Plug, Bitfinity, NFID, AstroX ME

Get Some $ICP

To acquire $CLOWN, you must exchange $ICP tokens. If you don't have any $ICP, you can either purchase directly from a crypto exchange, or transfer $ICP from another wallet

Swap $ICP for $CLOWN

Access ICPSwap DEX by visiting app.icpswap.com. Connect your wallet, swap $ICP for $CLOWN and confirm the transaction if prompted by your wallet
